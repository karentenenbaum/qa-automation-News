Tomato growing cheat sheet: Sign up for the Garden Party newsletter

Learn how to grow great tomatoes with our Garden Party newsletter, a cheat sheet that will tell you what you need to know for getting the most out of your plants this season.

By ALEX GROVES | erasmussen@scng.com | | The Press-Enterprise
PUBLISHED: February 18, 2021 at 6:55 am | UPDATED: February 26, 2021 at 10:35 am

Can’t wait to hop on the pandemic gardening train by planting some tomatoes this spring? Want to know how to grow your favorite tomato varieties even better? The Southern California News Group is putting out a newsletter all about those delicious fruits that are just waiting to be sliced into salads, made into sauce and put in that feta tomato pasta that’s gone viral.

Garden Party is an eight week course-style newsletter to all things tomatoes. Consider it your cheat sheet to growing great tomatoes.

Here’s how it works:

1. Visit our newsletters page and sign up for Garden Party. It’s the one with the tomato on it. (If you don’t have an account, you will need to make one, but don’t worry, it’s free.)

2. You’ll get a welcome email from us soon to let you know what to expect. Then we’ll start sending you emails once per week for eight weeks beginning in mid-March (when you really start seeing tomato plants in nurseries).

3. We’ll take you through all the important steps of the tomato growing process, from what you need to know before you visit the nursery to what to do once you have fruit. We’ll tell you how to space your tomato plants out; how often you should water your tomato plants; how to deal with the bugs and animals that might eat your tomato plants; and what it means if your tomato plant is yellow, spotted or has flowers falling off — and those are just a few of the things we’ll go over.

Sign up for Garden Party

Seeing this article after March? Don’t sweat it. You’ll receive the first of the eight newsletters after whenever you sign up.Newsletter signups will be open through early October 2021 to maximize the growing time here in Southern California.

After you sign up for Garden Party, don’t forget to check out some of our long-running entertainment newsletters, including Festival Pass, Park Life and Casino Insider.

Thanks for signing up , and until then, happy gardening!
